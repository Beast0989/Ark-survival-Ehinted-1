About Scortched Earth

Scortched Earth is a type of earth that has become desolate and hot. very very hot. This earth has become a giant desert that is infested with huge sand worms! You might be lucky and find some water but that is extremely rare. Good luck survivng in this wasteland, cause you might need to get used to the heat!

Bosses in Scortched Earth

If you want to escape scortched earth you will need to defeat the guardian of the desert. THE MANTICORE. The manticore is a huge lion with bat wings and a scorpian tale. It also have fiery posion breath. So good luck defeating this horrid beast if you want to escape. You might need help from the world's animals.

Scortched Earth's specific lifeforms

The animals of this earth evolved to survive in the hot sands of Scortched earth. The first specific animal is the Jerboa. The jerboa is a cute little racoon type thingy that can help the surivor predict the weather. These little cute bears are perfect for predicting the horrible weather in scortched earth.

The next animal is the Morrellatops. The morrellatops is a huge camel like creature that is much bigger than the human. This gaint lummocks can carry many litres of water that is uself for exploring the barren landscapes of the desert.

The final creature, and the scariest one, is the WYVERN. The wyvern is a massive dragon like beast that has two legs and huge spiky wings. These beasts live in a massive volvanic crack in the ground called the wyvern trench. There are 3 types of wyverns. Posion, fire and lightning. These 3 types spit you guessed it fire, lightning and posion. It will be a great challange taming these beasts though, watch out!
